Mobile App Pulse Monitor

About the App

Pulse Monitor helps users track pulse, HRV, breathing, and self-check protocols using smartphone signals.

You can use the app to measure and review:

  • Pulse and HRV using camera-based PPG measurement.
  • Breathing rate and breathing variability using motion sensors.
  • Tests, breathing practices, history, tags, and share cards.

For better accuracy:

  • Measure under similar conditions and stay still during recording.
  • Look at trends across multiple sessions rather than relying on a single value.

Pulse Monitor is provided for informational purposes only and is not a medical device.

Privacy Policy

Measurements are processed on the device. We do not send camera frames, raw motion signals, or measurement content to analytics services or remote calculation services.

Permissions

  • The camera is used only to measure pulse through fingertip-based detection.
  • Motion sensors are used only for breathing measurements and tests.

Data Storage

  • History, tags, and calculated metrics are stored locally on the device.
  • A result card is created and shared only through the Android system share sheet when you choose the Share action.
  • Records can be deleted inside the app.

Account

No account is required to use the app.

Medical Disclaimer

This application is not a medical device. Results are provided for informational purposes only. For diagnosis or treatment, please consult a qualified physician.
